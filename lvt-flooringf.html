About LVT Flooring

Expert Flooring Solutions In UAE

AFT provides premium Luxury Vinyl Tile (LVT) flooring solutions designed for contemporary offices, homes, and commercial interiors. Combining style, strength, and functionality, our LVT floors replicate the natural beauty of wood and stone while offering exceptional durability and comfort underfoot.

Each tile is built to withstand heavy use and resist wear, moisture, and stains, ensuring long-term performance in dynamic environments. Easy to install and maintain, our LVT flooring delivers a refined aesthetic and practical advantage, creating spaces that are both elegant and enduring.
