Cement HPL Raised Flooring

AFT's Steel Cement Core Anti-Static HPL Finished Raised Flooring Panels are engineered to meet the rigorous demands of modern commercial and technical environments. These panels feature a robust steel shell filled with lightweight cement, providing exceptional load-bearing capacity, fire resistance, and acoustic insulation. The high-pressure laminate (HPL) surface offers a durable, anti-static finish, effectively dissipating electrostatic charges to protect sensitive electronic equipment. Trimmed with conductive rubber edges, these panels ensure a seamless and secure installation.

Ideal for applications such as data centers, control rooms, server rooms, and office spaces, these panels facilitate efficient cable management, HVAC integration, and electrical routing beneath the floor. Their flame-retardant properties and durability make them a reliable choice for both new installations and retrofits. The panels are designed for easy installation and maintenance, ensuring long-term performance and adaptability in dynamic commercial and technical environments.
